♦ 4 tips for getting the best from your cleaner

  1. Ensure your house is in order before your cleaner arrives. She will have more time to focus on cleaning rather than clearing.
  2. Tell your cleaner what you want and what you expect. This way they can focus on what you really want rather than what they assume you want.
  3. Do not be afraid to complain. We would rather you told us so we can put things right before they become an issue.
  4. Praise and support. If your cleaning does something particularly well, tell them. Then we can put this into our routine in other houses.

♦ How to choose a great cleaner

When you are employing a cleaner, whether it’s through a company or directly, follow these tips to help you choose a great cleaner.

  1. Make sure you meet the person who the cleaner is directly responsible to. This face-to-face contact enables you to judge whether you trust the cleaning company and the cleaner. Don’t forget – if you have any issues you’ll want to take it up with the company, not the cleaner.
  2. Take up at least 3 references. A good cleaner will have a good track record. If the cleaner is new, and that’s not uncommon, then take up some references about the cleaning company the cleaner works for.
  3. Make sure the cleaner is fit. Cleaning is a demanding and physical job. You can’t clean if you can’t get up the stairs.
  4. Communication. You need to be able to communicate your needs to the cleaner, and if anything happens, the cleaner need to be able to communicate with you.
