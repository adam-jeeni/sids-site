About Us

A family business built on our reputation

The Cleaning Company specialises in domestic cleaning, commercial cleaning, one off cleaning and deep cleaning.

The success to our cleaning services is our reputation both from our clients and our staff. We endeavor to make sure our customers are 100% satified with our services and we do this via a program of staff training and customer engagement.

Cleaning Services Guaranteed Quality

We've been growing our business year on year since 2004. All of this growth has been organic in that new customers join us via referrals from current and old customers. We have ingrained in our cleaning service feed-back systems so we can continuously improve our services to you.

This enables us to guarantee that you will be fully satisfied with our service and that we can guarantee value for money.

Our Guaranteed...

We fully guarantee that our cleaning services will meet or exceed your expectation. As we grow our business our reputation is our golden card. Therefore we put our staff on a 3 week training program.
